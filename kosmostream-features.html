Real-Time Biodynamic Calendar

A live, day-by-day planting guide updated with current lunar phases, zodiac transits, and biodynamic root/flower/fruit/leaf day classifications. Never guess the right day to sow again.

Biodynamic Day Type Guide

Free-tier watering suggestions based on the biodynamic calendar and your local weather forecast. Know when to water, when to hold back, and why — without any guesswork.
